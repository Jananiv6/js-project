Introduction to
Task
Management

Task management is the process of overseeing a task through its lifecycle. It involves planning,
testing, tracking, and reporting. Task management can help individuals achieve goals or enable
groups of individuals to collaborate and share knowledge for the accomplishment of collective
goals. Tasks are also differentiated by complexity, from low to high.
