Manage your Tasks

Task management is the process of managing a task through its life cycle. It involves planning, testing, tracking, and reporting. Task management can help either individual achieve goals or groups of individuals collaborate and share knowledge for the accomplishment of collective goals. Tasks are also differentiated by complexity, from low to high.

What is Task Management?

Task management is more than a task manager checking off items on a to-do list! It’s an organized system for identifying, monitoring, and managing the work you and your team. Task management involves tracking task progress from start to finish, delegating work to team members, and setting deadlines to ensure projects are completed on time.
